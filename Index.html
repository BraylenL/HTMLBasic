My Favorite Hobby!

My favorite hobby is playing volleyball.

Why Volleyball?

There are many benefits to volleyball

  • It can increase your jumping height
  • Increases skill development
  • Allows for a faster reaction time

Disadvantages of Volleyball

Volleyball can cause many things to happen with your body

  1. Can cause pain to joints
  2. Muscle soreness/pain
  3. Can or may cause injury

Volleyball in General

Sometimes, volleyball is not really consided a popular sport. Most people will think of volleyball as a woman's sport, but volleyball is more than that. It is a sport that all people can play. It's probably one of the best sports that has ever EXISTED!
